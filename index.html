Altman & Berg’s Rationale

Altman & Berg’s objective is to publish valuable and actionable information about sales and marketing of the wine industry. These articles provide a mix of research studies, cross-industries analysis, marketing strategies, and commentary from diverse professionals in the industry. Regular readers are winery professionals involved in sales and marketing. Nonetheless any professional of the industry from distributors, merchants, and journalists can obtain valuable information from this publication.
